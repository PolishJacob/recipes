Ingredients
-----------
Avocado
Lime
Salt
Tomato

Instructions
------------
Mash the avocado in a bowl.
Add lime juice and salt.
Chop the tomato and mix it in.
Taste and adjust seasoning.
Serve and enjoy.
* To perserve the guacamole to enjoy for more than a day adding a good amount
of olive oil helps the avocado from turning brown!
